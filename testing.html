For almost eight years, I've been on a journey with web development that started in senior high school. My passion for coding pushed me to learn constantly, even while juggling school and other responsibilities.

A significant part of my story involves the hardware I started with. I worked on a laptop with a broken hard drive connector and only 2GB of RAM, forcing me to use my phone as a display for my web apps. These limitations became my greatest teacher. To make progress, I embraced the command line, switched from Windows to Arch Linux, and became proficient in system optimization.

This path honed my skills in creating highly efficient, mobile-first applications and gave me a powerful understanding of performance from the ground up. While I love building my own tools with bash scripts, my core passion remains creating streamlined, user-friendly web experiences.
